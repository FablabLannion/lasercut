## Case for 30-notes music box movement

CC-BY-SA Shadocko
The case is to be cut in 5mm plywood or equivalent.

## Settings used on Keyland laser cutter

Black layer:
- mode: cut
- power: 45W
- speed: 15mm/s

Red layer:
- mode: scan (fill)
- power: 40W
- speed: 150mm/s
- scan interval: 0.1mm
